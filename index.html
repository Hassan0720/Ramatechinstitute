About Rama Technical Institute

Rama Technical Institute is a leading institute which provides complete study material and total practical training. Classes are handled by highly qualified and experienced instructors.

The diploma course includes 1 hour theory and 2 hours practical sessions. Fast track courses are available for students who want to complete their course in just 6 months.

Students become capable of assembling computers within 3 to 4 months. They also learn repairing and chip-level fault detection with real practical exposure.

The institute continuously updates its training programs according to latest technology trends.
